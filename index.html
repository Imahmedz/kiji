Welcome to Kiji

Kiji helps you stay focused in your writing by eliminating all the distraction, and replacing it with a clean and minimalist screen.

If you want to switch to night mode, or go full-screen, you can use the tools in the right. (More tools coming soon)

To start typing, just delete this text, and change it with your own ;)

For help and open source info, please click the symbol on the bottom right of your screen :)
